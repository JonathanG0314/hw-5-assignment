Chupacabra

Sep 25th, 2009

Although stuffed and nailed to a board, the creature looks ready to spring to life and chomp off the nearest human finger. Its nose scrunches into a snarl. Its skinny frame implies it died hungry. Its gray, leathery skin sprouts course patches of fur. It looks sort of like a coyote, wolf or fox but not quite like any of them.

It's not one to mess with.
